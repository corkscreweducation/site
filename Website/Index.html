Our Mission

Welcome to Corkscrew Oaks Academy. We invite you to embark on an educational journey with us.

Here we are committed to providing a personalized and engaging homeschooling experience for young minds. Headmaster Gable's dedication to nurturing the potential within each student drives our mission.

Our curriculum, designed for Grades 1-5, places a strong emphasis on English Language Arts (ELA), social interaction, and practical life skills. We firmly believe that a solid foundation in these areas equips students for a lifetime of success.

Your child's unique learning journey is important to us. We tailor our "Core" hour to your specifications, using the chosen homeschool curriculum of your choice. If you're looking for core curriculum options, we offer inspiring choices that spark curiosity and ignite a passion for learning.

You'll witness results, as your child flourishes academically and personally. Together, we'll pave the path to a brighter future.

Content Overview

The Explode the Code workbook series from Educators Publishing Service (EPS) is a fantastic homeschool phonics series for young children. Explode the Code (2nd Edition) is a researched-based, multisensory, Orton-Gillingham based program that builds essential literacy skills through direct, systematic, phonics instruction; it teaches students the skills they need to read words, phrases, sentences, and stories on their own.

Saxon Math is the top choice for homeschoolers when it comes to math curriculum. Saxon's incremental approach is one of the most effective teaching methods; learning pieces of information in small amounts, and then continually repeating concepts keeps knowledge and skills fresh.

The Good and the Beautiful is leading a movement to return children to a love of wholesome literature that strengthens families and that creates beautiful hearts and minds.

Campfire Curriculums is made up of engaging lesson plans for those who crave more ideas. It's projects incorporate auditory, tactile, kinesthetic, and visual learning styles.

Values


English Mastery: In 2023, a strong command of the English language is crucial for success. We empower students with language skills that can defuse hostile situations, capture attention of others, and unlock limitless possibilities in the age of AI.
Athletics & Art: Our holistic approach includes exposure to sports, creative arts, and crafts, igniting a passion for physical and creative expression.
Practical Skills: We require students to cook their own meals during lunch. Cooking materials & instruction are provided.
1-1 Attention: In order for each child to get the attention they need, our class capacity is 8 students.
